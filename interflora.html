The challenge

In 2018 Interflora faced a change of ownership following a three year period with declining revenue and scarce investment in data initiatives despite owning a strong digital platform. The new leadership sought to accelerate growth through digital transformation and Responsive was tasked with improving data infrastructure and customer experience to fuel growth.

The solution

Insourcing key competencies and selecting the right strategic partners to best supports agile workflow, while improving digital infrastructure to expand permission data base through first part data. This was used to implement AI-driven CX initiatives including an advanced product recommendation model and robotics development.
