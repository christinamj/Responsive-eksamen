The challenge

After a three year period of zero growth combined with a continuous decline in stock value, Matas needed help with a digital transformation – seeking to win back customers from digital players in the market. As part of a new digitalisation strategy Responsive was tasked with developing part of the new strategy. This included outlining a new CX strategy, revamping critical infrastructure and developing customer facing digital platforms as well as helping with the execution of critical elements of the digitalisation journey.

The solution

Developing a new Club Matas App, a new loyalty concept and updated benefits to create an easy and effortless customer experience. This was combined with the development of new streams of revenue to give additional customer benefits, which was developed in a set-up that ensured easy further development and operation by Matas in-house. In addition, a SoW model, a suppression framework, a STO model and an AI cloud platform was also implemented
